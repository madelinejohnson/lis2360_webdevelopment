Sephora's Virtual Artist

Artificial Intelligence allows consumers to try on makeup through the Sephora app. This reduces the need to visit the store in person, thus contributing to the rise in E-Commerce.

Echo Look!

Amazon has created their first “style assistant”. This technology analyzes your outfit through a series of algorithms, and then lets you know if you look "good" based on various fashion criteria.

Visual Search Technology

Picture this: your favorite shirt finally bit the dust, but the store doesn't sell it anymore. Well, with visual search technology, all you have to do is upload a photo of that shirt, and then AI technology analyzes it. You will then be shown the closest product the website has.
